# Privacy Policy for WordDex

**Last Updated: December 1, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to WordDex! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our language learning flashcard application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using WordDex, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Collected when you create an account directly, or through Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In
- **Authentication Data**: Provided by third-party authentication services (Google, Apple)

### User-Generated Content
- **Flashcards**: Text content, translations, and vocabulary items you create
- **Images**: Photos you capture or upload for text recognition and flashcard creation
- **Decks**: Collections and organization of your flashcards
- **Study Progress**: Your learning progress and flashcard review history

### Technical Information
- **API Usage Logs**: Records of API calls made to manage service limits and ensure fair usage
- **Device Information**: Basic device information necessary for app functionality

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the WordDex service:

- **Account Management**: To create and maintain your account
- **Flashcard Functionality**: To store, organize, and sync your flashcards across devices
- **Text Recognition**: To process images and extract text for flashcard creation
- **Language Processing**: To provide translations and language learning content
- **Service Quality**: To monitor API usage and maintain service quality

## Third-Party Services

WordDex uses the following third-party services to provide functionality:

### Data Storage and Authentication
- **Supabase**: Securely stores your account information, flashcards, and app data
  - Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

### AI and Image Processing
- **Anthropic Claude API**: Processes text for translations, definitions, and language learning content
  - Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
- **Google Cloud Vision API**: Processes images to recognize and extract text
  - Privacy Policy: https://cloud.google.com/vision/docs/data-usage

### Authentication Services
- **Google Sign-In**: Optional authentication method
  - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Apple Sign-In**: Optional authentication method
  - Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

**Important Note**: When you use text recognition features, your images are sent to Claude API and Google Cloud Vision API for processing. These services process the images to extract text but do not store your images permanently. Please review their privacy policies for more information.

## Data Security

We take data security seriously:

- All data is transmitted using secure HTTPS encryption
- Authentication is handled through secure, industry-standard protocols
- Your passwords are never stored in plain text
- Access to your data is protected by authentication

## Data Retention and Deletion

- **Active Accounts**: Your data is stored securely while your account is active
- **Account Deletion**: You can delete your account at any time through the app settings
- **Immediate Deletion**: When you delete your account, all your personal data, flashcards, images, and associated information are immediately and permanently deleted from our servers
- **No Data Retention**: We do not retain any of your data after account deletion

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access**: View all your personal data and flashcards within the app
- **Export**: Export your flashcard data at any time
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data through the app settings
- **Correction**: Edit or update your flashcards and data at any time
- **Withdrawal**: Delete your account to withdraw consent for data processing

## Data Sharing

We do not:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Track your activity for advertising

We only share data with the third-party services listed above, and only to the extent necessary to provide app functionality.

## Children's Privacy

WordDex is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located in different countries. By using WordDex, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes:

- We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- We will notify you through the app or via email if the changes are significant
- Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

We respect your privacy and do not track your behavior for marketing or advertising purposes.
